About Me

I am Xuefeng Hu, a Ph.D. student at Computer Science Department @ the University of South California, advised by Prof. Ram Nevatia.
My current research interests include Computer Vision, Multi-Modality Learning, and Automatic Story Comprehension.
I obtained my B.S. degrees at both the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor and the Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and an M.S. degree at the Columbia University in the city of New York. I worked as a graduate student researcher with Prof. Shih-Fu Chang and Dr. Svebor Karaman in the DVMM Lab during my time at Columbia.
